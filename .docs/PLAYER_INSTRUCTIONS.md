# How to Play Bang!

## Game Overview

Bang! is a Wild West card game for 4-7 players where you take on secret roles (Sheriff, Deputies, Outlaws, Renegade) and battle to be the last team standing. Each player also has a unique character with special abilities.

## Setup

1. **Roles are Assigned:**
   - **Sheriff:** Revealed to all (gets +1 health)
   - **Deputies:** Hidden, help the Sheriff
   - **Outlaws:** Hidden, try to kill the Sheriff
   - **Renegade:** Hidden, wants to be last player standing with Sheriff

2. **Character Selection:**
   - Each player gets 2 random characters to choose from
   - Select your character (determines starting health and special ability)

3. **Starting Hand:**
   - Draw cards equal to your health
   - Sheriff draws +1 card (total health + 1)

## Turn Structure

Each turn has 3 phases:

### 1. Draw Phase
- **Draw 2 cards** from the deck (REQUIRED first)
- Some characters have special draw abilities

### 2. Action Phase
- **Play any number of cards** from your hand
- **Play up to 1 BANG!** per turn (unless you have Volcanic or are Willy the Kid)
- **Equip weapons and equipment**
- **Use healing cards (Beer, Saloon)**

### 3. Discard Phase
- **Discard down to your health** if you have too many cards
- Cannot have more cards in hand than current health points

## Card Types

### Brown Border Cards (Play Once, Then Discard)

#### Offensive Cards
- **BANG!** - Attack a player within range
  - Target must play Missed! or lose 1 health
  - Can only play 1 per turn (unless you have Volcanic)

- **Gatling** - BANG! all other players at once

- **Indians** - All players must play BANG! or lose 1 health

- **Duel** - Challenge a player to a BANG! battle
  - Players alternate playing BANG! cards
  - First player who can't loses 1 health

#### Defensive Cards
- **Missed!** - Dodge a BANG! attack
  - Can only be played in response to BANG!

#### Draw Cards
- **Stagecoach** - Draw 2 cards
- **Wells Fargo** - Draw 3 cards
- **General Store** - Reveal cards equal to players, each picks one

#### Stealing/Discarding
- **Panic** - Steal a card from a player at distance 1
- **Cat Balou** - Make any player discard a card

#### Healing
- **Beer** - Heal 1 health
  - Cannot exceed max health
  - Cannot be played with only 2 players alive

- **Saloon** - All players heal 1 health

### Blue Border Cards (Equipment - Stays in Play)

#### Weapons
- **Colt .45** - Default weapon (range 1)
- **Volcanic** - Range 1, unlimited BANGs per turn
- **Schofield** - Range 2
- **Remington** - Range 3
- **Rev Carabine** - Range 4
- **Winchester** - Range 5

**Note:** You can only have 1 weapon at a time. New weapon replaces old one.

#### Defense Equipment
- **Barrel** - When targeted by BANG!, draw a card
  - If it's Hearts, the BANG! misses automatically!

- **Mustang** - Other players see you at +1 distance

- **Scope** - You see all players at -1 distance

#### Special Cards
- **Dynamite** - Place in front of you
  - At start of your turn, draw a card
  - If Spades 2-9, lose 3 health!
  - Otherwise, pass dynamite to next player

- **Jail** - Place in front of another player (not Sheriff)
  - At start of their turn, draw a card
  - If not Hearts, skip their turn!

## Distance and Range

**Distance** is how far apart players are:
- Counted by player positions (minimum 1)
- Modified by Mustang (+1), Scope (-1), and character abilities

**Range** is how far you can shoot:
- Default Colt .45: range 1 (adjacent players)
- Better weapons have longer range
- You can only BANG! players within your weapon's range

## Character Abilities

Each character has a unique ability:

### Passive Abilities
- **Willy the Kid** - Unlimited BANGs per turn
- **Calamity Janet** - Can use BANG! as Missed! and vice versa
- **Paul Regret** - Others see you at +1 distance
- **Rose Doolan** - You see others at -1 distance
- **Jourdonnais** - Has a virtual Barrel (always active)
- **Slab the Killer** - Your targets need 2 Missed! to dodge

### Draw Phase Abilities
- **Black Jack** - Show 2nd drawn card, if red draw 3rd
- **Kit Carlson** - Look at top 3, choose 2 to draw
- **Jesse Jones** - Can draw 1st card from another player's hand
- **Pedro Ramirez** - Can draw 1st card from discard pile
- **Lucky Duke** - Draw 2 cards for "draw!" effects, choose 1

### Triggered Abilities
- **Bart Cassidy** - Draw 1 card for each health lost
- **El Gringo** - When hit, draw from attacker's hand
- **Suzy Lafayette** - When hand is empty, draw 1 card
- **Vulture Sam** - Take all cards from eliminated players

### Manual Abilities
- **Sid Ketchum** - Discard 2 cards to heal 1 health

## Winning Conditions

### Sheriff and Deputies Win
- All Outlaws and the Renegade are eliminated

### Outlaws Win
- The Sheriff is eliminated

### Renegade Wins
- Only Sheriff and Renegade remain
- Renegade then kills Sheriff

## Strategy Tips

### As Sheriff
- Reveal your identity immediately
- Protect suspected Deputies
- Watch for Outlaws attacking you
- Remember: If you kill a Deputy, you lose all your cards!

### As Deputy
- Protect the Sheriff without revealing yourself too early
- Help eliminate Outlaws
- Watch for the Renegade

### As Outlaw
- Stay hidden initially
- Coordinate attacks on Sheriff
- Eliminate Deputies first if possible
- Reward: Draw 3 cards when you eliminate someone

### As Renegade
- Play both sides
- Help Sheriff eliminate Outlaws early
- Eliminate Deputies when convenient
- Ensure Sheriff is last opponent standing

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Playing cards before drawing** - You MUST draw 2 cards first!

2. **Playing multiple BANGs** - Only 1 per turn (unless Volcanic/Willy)

3. **Forgetting distance** - Check range before playing BANG!

4. **Not using character abilities** - Remember your special power!

5. **Sheriff killing Deputies** - Massive penalty (lose all cards)

6. **Beer with 2 players** - Cannot be played in final duel

7. **Holding too many cards** - Must discard to health at end of turn

## Troubleshooting

**"Can't play this card"**
- Did you draw 2 cards first?
- Is the target in range?
- Have you already played BANG! this turn?

**"No valid targets"**
- Check weapon range vs. distance
- All players might be out of range
- Try equipping a better weapon

**"Must discard cards"**
- You have more cards than health
- Select cards to discard until hand = health

## Have Fun!

Bang! is about bluffing, deduction, and quick thinking. Enjoy the Wild West showdown!
